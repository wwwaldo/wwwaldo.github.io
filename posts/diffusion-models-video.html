Diffusion Models for Video Generation

Date: June 30, 2024 | Estimated Reading Time: 25 min | Author: Lilian Weng

Diffusion models have demonstrated strong results on image synthesis in past years. Now the research community is extending them to video generation, which introduces new challenges in maintaining temporal consistency and managing computational resources across frames.

Key Challenges in Video Diffusion

Video generation poses unique challenges compared to image generation:

  • Temporal consistency between frames
  • Computational complexity scaling with video length
  • Memory constraints when processing multiple frames
  • Motion modeling and physics consistency
